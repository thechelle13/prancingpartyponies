About Us

Welcome to Prancing Party Ponies!

We are a family-owned business passionate about creating magical moments for children through the joy of pony riding. Our gentle, well-trained ponies and experienced staff ensure a safe and delightful experience for riders of all skill levels.

Whether you're planning a birthday party, a school event, or just a fun weekend activity, we bring our ponies to you or welcome you to our friendly farm setting.
